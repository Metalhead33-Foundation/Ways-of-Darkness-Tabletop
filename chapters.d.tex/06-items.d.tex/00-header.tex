\chapter{Common Item Types}

Although this ruleset is meant to provide merely the basic rules, templates and game mechanics - leaving the definitions and listings of actual races, backgrounds, items, etc. relevant to the setting to the various modules - there are some particularly common types of items \Parentheses{weapons, armour, etc.} where leaving the defintions of items to the modules would cause redundancy.

Thus, in this chapter, some particularly common items will be defined, along with several item templates.
