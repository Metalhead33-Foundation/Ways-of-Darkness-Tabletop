\textit{Crushing:} $\frac{1d4 \pm modifiers}{5}*Strength$
}{A \textbf{pollaxe} - or alternatively a \textbf{halberd} - is basically the combination of an axe and a spear - or more precisely, an axe with an enlonged handle fitting for a spear, with a spike at the very end of the weapon, allowing to fufill the function of both the spear and the axe.}
